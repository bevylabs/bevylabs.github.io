Automated Water Control and Garden Irrigation System

  • July 23, 2018
    • •
  • Wordpress, Ghost
    • •
  • Jane Doe

In large scale-farming, the proper supply of water for the plants is the most labor-intensive and time-consuming task. In order to make work easier and more efficient, an automated Irrigation system is needed. Automated water supply system will keep the water level in the tank within a user-selected range by filling the tank with user preferred source. Whenever the soil moisture is lower than the particular level the system will automatically supply water to plants. Using the mobile App, the user can monitor and control all of the above procedures.
